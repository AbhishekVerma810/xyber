We’ve got answers for your customers. And we’ve got answers for you too. Here are our FAQs:

What kind of business types can Answer AI help?

Virtually any! If your business has an online presence and talks to customers, Answer AI has a role to play. From small businesses to larger enterprises – and whether your website is built for e-commerce, education or entertainment – automated conversations can add value and save you time and money.

Can Answer AI speak other languages besides English?

Oui, ja, sì! Answer AI is highly proficient at understanding and responding to communication in a selection of major European languages. Allowing you to connect more easily with overseas customers if your business needs to.

How much does it cost?

Our pricing is super-simple and scales according to your business’s needs – you can see our prices here. Whichever package you choose, Answer AI has the power to deliver big cost reductions for your business thanks to the customer service time and resource savings it brings.

Can I try Answer AI for free before committing?

Yes, you can sign up for the free plan to have a play with Answer AI, and to see how it might work for your business. If you like it, you have the option of upgrading to our Standard, Premium or Enterprise packages to access the app’s full features, additional support and added control.

What happens when Answer AI can’t help a customer?

Answer AI is a powerful tool and can be trained to deal with a virtually limitless range of enquiries. But there might be situations that only your team can resolve – or times when a customer really wants to talk to a human. In these situations, you can set up Answer AI to channel enquiries to a live assistant or capture the customers query which is then passed to your email or support system

Can Answer AI deal with enquiries about product orders?

It certainly can. Answer AI does more than just issue universally applicable information – it can actively connect with individual people’s details to address user-specific enquiries. It integrates quickly and easily with platforms like Shopify to give accurate, secure, up-to-the-moment updates and guidance to customers.
